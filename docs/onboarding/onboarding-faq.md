## Onboarding FAQ
=====================

### Account Approval Timeframe

The account approval process typically takes between 24 to 72 hours. During this time, our team will review your application and ensure that you meet all the required criteria.

If you haven't received an approval email within this timeframe, please contact us at [support@devapi.com](mailto:support@devapi.com) with your account username or email address associated with your application. We'll respond with a confirmation of receipt and provide an estimated date for approval.

### Free Tier Limitations

Our free tier allows 10 API requests per minute and 100,000 total requests per day. If you exceed these limits, you will receive a "Rate Limit Exceeded" error (HTTP status code 429) with the following message:
```
Rate Limit Exceeded
Maximum rate limit reached. Please contact support to upgrade your plan.
```
To avoid this error, please adjust your application to slow down API requests or consider upgrading to a paid tier.

### Getting Help

If you're having trouble with our API or need assistance with your account, we offer several support options:

*   **Email Support**: Reach us at [support@devapi.com](mailto:support@devapi.com) for email support. Please include your account username and a brief description of the issue in the subject line.
*   **API Documentation**: Our official API documentation is available at [docs.devapi.com](http://docs.devapi.com). It includes detailed examples, code snippets, and error codes.
*   **Live Chat**: During business hours (Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 5 PM EST), you can initiate a live chat session with our support team. Simply click the "Chat Now" button on our website.

### Service Level Agreement (SLA) during Trial

During your trial period, we guarantee a response time of no more than 2 hours for all support requests. If we fail to meet this promise, you'll receive a credit of $100 USD towards your next bill.

Keep in mind that response times may vary depending on the complexity of the issue and our workload. We appreciate your patience and understanding during this time.

### Error Codes

Here are some common error codes you might encounter:

*   `404`: Not Found
    *   Reason: The requested API endpoint or resource was not found.
    *   Solution: Check the API documentation for the correct endpoint URL or consult with our support team.
*   `429`: Rate Limit Exceeded
    *   Reason: You exceeded the rate limit for a particular API request or endpoint.
    *   Solution: Adjust your application to slow down requests or consider upgrading to a paid tier.

If you have any further questions or concerns, please don't hesitate to reach out to us at [support@devapi.com](mailto:support@devapi.com).
